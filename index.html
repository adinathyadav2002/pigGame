Pig Dice Game Rules

Objective :Be the first player to reach 100 points.

Gameplay

  1. Starting the Game: Choose a player to go first.
  2. Player's Turn:
    • Roll the die as many times as you want.
    • Add the numbers you roll to your turn total.
    • If you roll a 1, your turn ends and you score no points for this turn.
    • If you roll a 2-6, you can choose to roll again or hold.
  3. Ending the Turn:
    • If you hold, add your turn total to your overall score.
    • Pass the die to the next player.
  4. Winning the Game: The first player to reach 100 points wins.
